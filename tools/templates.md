# OpenAGI Beta Testing Templates

This directory contains templates and forms for managing the OpenAGI beta testing program.

## 📋 Application Form Template

Use this template to create online application forms (Google Forms, Typeform, etc.):

### Beta Tester Application Form

**Welcome to the OpenAGI Beta Testing Program!**

Help us build the most comprehensive AI platform with 14,000+ features across Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Audio Processing, and more.

---

#### Personal Information
- **Full Name** *(required)*
- **Email Address** *(required)*
- **GitHub Username** *(optional)*
- **LinkedIn Profile** *(optional)*

#### Professional Background
- **Current Job Title** *(required)*
- **Company/Organization** *(required)*
- **Years of AI/ML Experience** *(dropdown: 0-1, 2-3, 4-6, 7-10, 10+ years)*
- **Primary Areas of Expertise** *(checkboxes: NLP, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Data Science, Audio Processing, Automation, Web Scraping, API Development, DevOps, Research)*

#### Technical Experience
- **Describe your AI/ML experience** *(text area, required)*
  - What AI/ML tools and frameworks have you used?
  - What projects have you worked on?
  - What's your experience level with different AI domains?

- **Programming Languages** *(checkboxes: Python, R, Julia, JavaScript, Java, C++, Go, Scala, Other)*

- **AI/ML Frameworks Used** *(checkboxes: TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, Keras, Hugging Face, OpenCV, spaCy, NLTK, LangChain, Other)*

- **Previous Beta Testing Experience** *(text area)*
  - Have you participated in beta testing for AI/ML platforms before?
  - What was your experience like?

#### Availability and Preferences
- **How many hours per week can you dedicate to testing?** *(dropdown: 1-2, 3-4, 5-6, 7-10, 10+ hours)*

- **Which OpenAGI feature categories interest you most?** *(checkboxes: NLP, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Audio Processing, Data Analysis, Automation, Web Scraping, API Integrations, Developer Tools, Image Generation, Text Generation, Prediction Models, Optimization)*

- **Preferred communication channel** *(radio: Discord, Slack, Email, Telegram)*

- **Timezone** *(dropdown)*

#### Motivation and Goals
- **Why do you want to join the OpenAGI beta testing program?** *(text area, required)*

- **What specific OpenAGI features or capabilities are you most excited to test?** *(text area)*

- **What do you hope to achieve through beta testing?** *(text area)*
  - Learning new AI techniques
  - Contributing to open source
  - Building professional network
  - Getting early access to cutting-edge tools
  - Other (please specify)

#### Additional Information
- **Any additional comments or questions?** *(text area, optional)*

---

**By submitting this application, I agree to:**
- [ ] Provide constructive feedback on OpenAGI features
- [ ] Report bugs and issues in a timely manner
- [ ] Respect confidentiality of beta features
- [ ] Follow the testing guidelines and code of conduct
- [ ] Participate actively in the testing community

*Thank you for your interest in the OpenAGI Beta Testing Program! We'll review your application and get back to you within 5-7 business days.*
